unsigned CC_Mqtt5ConnectExtraConfig::m_receiveMaximum

"Receive Maximum" property - allowed amount of incomplete Qos1 and Qos2 publishes, defaults to 0, not added when 0. When equals to 0 (default) no property is added, which defaults to 65,535 on the broker side.
